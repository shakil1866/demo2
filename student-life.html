Faith & Values

Our character development program nurtures the whole child‚Äîintellectually, spiritually, and emotionally.

FAITH

Waterloo is a school by Christians who want each student to grow into flourishing young adulthood. Each student is on a unique, personal journey, and we meet and mentor them through their high school years. Waterloo may not look like other private Christian schools, but our faith is integral to everything we do.

OUR VALUES

Waterloo School integrates academics and city engagement, mentoring students toward wholeness in Christ. Our mission is animated by seven core values: Belonging, Mentorship, Restoration, Complexity, Flourishing, Growth, and Generosity, fostering a community where students mature through experience and service
