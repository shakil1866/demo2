About waterloo austin

Waterloo offers an innovative project-focused education that fosters deeper learning, more meaningful relationships, and lasting personal achievement.

Our Mission

"To prepare students to lead, innovate, and make a positive difference in an ever-changing world."

Waterloo’s mission is to integrate academics with meaningful engagement in the city, mentoring students to become whole people—in relationships and in Christ.

Through a community shaped by belonging, mentorship, growth, and generosity, we prepare students to flourish academically, personally, and spiritually while making a lasting impact on the world around them.

Explore Our Approach

Our Vision

Our vision is to cultivate a dynamic and faith-centered learning community where students are inspired to pursue academic excellence, develop strong character, and grow in wisdom and confidence.

We seek to equip young people to think critically, lead with integrity, serve generously, and use their unique gifts to create meaningful impact in their communities and the world.

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire." — W.B. Yeats

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do and every decision we make.

Community

Integrity

Innovation

Curiosity

Global Citizenship

Empathy

We develop understanding and compassion for others.

Resilience

Our Journey

Key milestones in the evolution of waterloo austin.

2015

Foundation

Waterloo was established with a vision to integrate academics, mentorship, and meaningful city engagement.

2017

First Graduating Class

Our first seniors graduated, achieving outstanding college acceptance results.

2018

Expansion of Programs

Introduced interdisciplinary projects, STEM labs, and city-based learning experiences.

2022

Academic Excellence

Recognized for innovation, small class mentorship, and strong student outcomes.

2024

Continuing to Flourish

Growing as a vibrant learning community committed to excellence, faith, and impact.
