Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common questions we receive.

We have rolling admissions, which means we accept applications throughout the year. However, we recommend applying by March 1 for the following academic year to ensure priority consideration for financial aid and scheduling.

We look for students who demonstrate strong academic potential and a genuine desire to learn. While we consider standardized test scores, we place equal weight on essays, recommendations, and the personal interview. We want to see curious, motivated students who will contribute to our community.

Yes! In addition to need-based financial aid, we offer merit scholarships for students who demonstrate exceptional achievement in academics, arts, athletics, or leadership. Scholarship amounts vary and are awarded during the admissions process.

Absolutely! We encourage all prospective families to visit our campus. We offer personalized tours Monday through Friday during school hours. You'll have the opportunity to observe classes, meet current students and faculty, and get a feel for life at waterloo austin. Schedule your visit by contacting our admissions office.

Our student-teacher ratio is approximately 10:1, with average class sizes of 15-20 students. This allows our teachers to know each student personally and provide individualized attention and support.

We offer over 50 clubs and activities including athletics, performing arts, robotics, debate, community service, and more. Students are also encouraged to start their own clubs based on their interests. Our athletics program competes at the varsity and junior varsity levels in multiple sports.
